LeanHRPT is an open-source software suite intended for receiving, demodulating, decoding, and processing data being broadcast by various Earth observation satellites.

Multiple satellites in orbit of Earth use a direct radio broadcast to transmit data gathered by their sensors and instruments in real time. This radio signal can be received by practically anyone with the correct equipment, and processed using LeanHRPT to obtain final image products.
